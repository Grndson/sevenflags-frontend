1870 West

Description

Rising 30 floors above the vibrant streets of Westlands, 1870 West is a bold residential landmark redefining luxury urban living in Nairobi. Positioned at the intersection of Lower Kabete and Peponi Roads just steps from the iconic Sarit Centre, this contemporary tower offers seamless access to Nairobi's finest retail, dining, and entertainment venues. The project's curvilinear architecture and panoramic green views over Westlands Park make it both a visual marvel and a peaceful retreat, even at street level.

Set in Nairobi's rapidly transforming second CBD, 1870 West combines cutting-edge design with unmatched investment potential. The top floor is entirely devoted to leisure, providing residents with a rooftop entertainment hub that overlooks both the city skyline and leafy suburbs. Surrounded by expanding infrastructure like the Nairobi Expressway and Westlands' urban regeneration efforts, this development stands as a beacon for both luxury living and strong rental yields crafted by the visionary minds behind Enaki.
