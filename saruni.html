The Saruni

Description

Nestled along the serene Riverside Drive, The Saruni is a contemporary development that offers an elevated living experience with unmatched convenience and style. Each apartment, ranging from 1 and 2-bedroom units to expansive 3-bedroom residences and luxurious 4-bedroom penthouses, is designed with careful attention to space, natural light, and top-tier finishes. Its prime location ensures easy access to Nairobi’s key districts, including Westlands, Kilimani, and the CBD, making it ideal for both professionals and families seeking urban comfort.

The Saruni features a refined collection of lifestyle amenities tailored for leisure and wellness. From stunning Sky Gardens on the 9th, 10th, 12th, and 13th floors to ground-level recreational spaces, every corner is crafted to inspire serenity and connection. With sweeping views of the Nairobi skyline, lush green pockets, and thoughtfully curated interiors, The Saruni is not just a home, it’s a sanctuary above the city.
